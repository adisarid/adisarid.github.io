Biography

Adi Sarid is a partner and head of Operations Research Department at the Sarid Research Institute LTD.

Adi is completing his PhD in Operations Research at the department of Industrial Engineering in Tel-Aviv university. Adi holds an MSc in Operations Research from Tel-Aviv university, and a BA in Mathematics Statistics and Operations Research from the Technion.

Adi is also a certified RStudio instructor, teaching tidyverse, shiny, statistics, and data science.
